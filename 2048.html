About 2048

2048 is a single-player sliding block puzzle game. Your goal is to combine numbered tiles on a grid to create the 2048 tile. It’s a simple yet addictive game that tests your logic and planning skills!

How to Play 2048

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Arrow Keys – Slide tiles in any direction

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎮 Start the Game – The game begins with two tiles on a 4x4 grid.
  • ➕ Combine Tiles – Swipe in any direction to slide all tiles. Matching numbers combine into one.
  • 🔢 Score Points – Each combination gives you points. Aim for high scores!
  • 🎯 Reach 2048 – Keep combining tiles until you create the 2048 tile.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🔒 Keep your highest tile in a corner and build around it.
  • 📏 Plan ahead — don’t just swipe randomly.
  • 🧹 Avoid filling up the board with random tiles — stay organized.
  • 🔁 Practice makes perfect — don’t give up after a few tries!
