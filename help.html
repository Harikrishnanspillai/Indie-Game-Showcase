About Help No Brake

Help No Brake is a chaotic and hilarious driving game where your vehicle has no brakes! Navigate challenging courses, avoid deadly obstacles, and try to survive the madness. It's fast, fun, and a wild ride from start to finish!

How to Play Help No Brake

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️ Arrow Keys – Steer left and right
  • ⏩ Up Arrow – Accelerate (no brakes!)
  • ␣ Space – Reset level (if stuck or flipped)
  • ⎋ Esc – Pause/Menu

🧩 How to Play

  • 🚗 Start Driving – Your car only goes forward. Fast.
  • ⚠️ Avoid Obstacles – Dodge spikes, pits, and sudden turns.
  • 🌀 Navigate the Track – Use sharp reflexes to stay on course.
  • 🔁 Restart When Needed – Don’t worry if you crash — try again!
  • 🏁 Reach the End – Survive each insane level to win!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🎮 Master cornering — drifting with no brakes is your only hope.
  • 🧠 Memorize level layouts to react quickly.
  • 💥 Crashed? Hit space to restart instantly and learn from your mistakes.
  • 📈 The faster you finish, the better your performance!
