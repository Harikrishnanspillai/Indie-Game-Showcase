About White Knuckle 🎮🚀

White Knuckle is a fast-paced, action-packed driving game where you must speed down a dangerous road filled with traps, hazards, and tight turns. Stay focused and react fast to survive!

How to Play 🕹️

    🕹️ Controls

    • ⬅️➡️ Use left and right arrow keys to steer your vehicle.
    • ⏫ Stay alert — quick reflexes are key!

    🚗 Gameplay Guide

    • 🛣️ Dodge Obstacles – Avoid hitting traps and barriers.
    • 🔥 Stay on the Road – Keep your car centered and balanced.
    • 🏁 Reach the Finish – Survive the chaos and make it through each stage.

    💡 Tips

    • ⚠️ Watch ahead — anticipate upcoming turns.
    • 🎧 Play with sound for an intense experience!
    • 🧠 Stay calm and time your moves precisely.
