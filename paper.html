About Paper.io 2

Paper.io 2 is an addictive multiplayer territory game where your goal is to conquer as much area as possible. Move your character around to enclose space and expand your territory. But beware — if someone touches your trail, it's game over!

How to Play Paper.io 2

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Arrow Keys – Move
  • 🖱️ Mouse (in some versions) – Control movement direction

🧩 How to Play

  • 🌍 Start the Game – You’ll appear on a shared map with other players.
  • 📏 Expand Your Area – Leave your base, circle around space, and return to add it to your zone.
  • ⚠️ Protect Your Trail – If another player hits your trail, you’re out!
  • 🏁 Aim for 100% – The more space you conquer, the higher your rank.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🎨 Play aggressively early to get a large zone quickly.
  • 🚫 Cut off enemies by hitting their trail.
  • 🌀 Don't stay outside your base too long — you’re vulnerable!
  • 🛡️ Stay near your territory edge to defend more easily.
