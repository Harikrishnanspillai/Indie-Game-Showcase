About Toasterball game

Toasterball is a chaotic physics-based sports game where you play as toasters trying to score goals by launching toast at a ball! Play solo or with friends in hilarious multiplayer duels full of surprises, traps, and buttery madness.

How to Play Toasterball!

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️ Use left/right arrow keys or controller to tilt your toaster.
  • 🍞 Hold and release to launch toast and hit the ball.

🧩 How to Play

  • ⚽ Score Goals – Knock the ball into the opponent’s goal to earn points.
  • 💥 Survive the Chaos – Deal with random hazards like lasers, bombs, and slippery butter floors!
  • 🏆 Win Matches – First to the required score wins the game!
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Play with Friends – Best enjoyed in 1v1 or 2v2 party mode!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🧈 Timing your toast shots matters more than spamming them.
  • 🎲 Embrace the randomness — each match feels different.
  • 🕹️ Try a controller for a smoother experience!
