About UNO

UNO is the classic card game that brings friends and families together for fast-paced, color-matching fun! The goal is to be the first to get rid of all your cards while playing strategically and using action cards to sabotage your opponents.

How to Play UNO

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Use your mouse to click and play cards.
  • 🎴 Click on a card that matches the color or number of the top card in the discard pile.
  • 🃏 Use action cards wisely (Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, Wild, and Wild Draw Four).

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎯 Match Cards – Match the top card by color or number.
  • 🚫 Use Action Cards – Play strategically to block others or force them to draw more cards.
  • 🔔 Call "UNO!" – When you have one card left, click the one button or you’ll be penalized.
  • 🏁 Win the Round – First player to get rid of all their cards wins the round!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🔄 Save your Wild and Draw Four cards for when you really need them.
  • 👀 Keep an eye on your opponents’ card counts.
  • 🎨 Use Wild cards to change colors and break combos.
