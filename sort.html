About Sort the Court

Sort the Court is a decision-making game where you play as a king or queen ruling over a kingdom. Your job is to respond to the requests of your citizens and mystical visitors with a simple "Yes" or "No" — but every choice has consequences!

How to Play Sort the Court

🕹️ Controls

  • ✅ Y – Say Yes
  • ❌ N – Say No
  • 🖱️ Mouse – Navigate the main menu

🧩 How to Play

  • 👑 Make Decisions – Respond "Yes" or "No" to each visitor's request.
  • 💰 Manage Resources – Keep track of gold, population, and happiness.
  • 🧙 Meet Unique Characters – From wizards to ghosts, each visitor brings something new!
  • 🏰 Grow Your Kingdom – Aim to keep your people happy and prosperous.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⚖️ Balance is key – Don’t focus only on one resource.
  • 🌟 Some decisions may seem risky but can lead to great rewards.
  • 📈 Pay attention to recurring characters — their stories evolve.
  • 🗓️ The kingdom evolves over days — survive as long as you can!
