About Mall of the Dead

Mall of the Dead is the infamous secret level (E4M9) in the cult-classic FPS Blood. Set in a zombie-ridden shopping mall inspired by *Dawn of the Dead*, this map blends intense horror-action, clever design, and deadly ambushes in a uniquely creepy environment.

How to Play Mall of the Dead

🕹️ Controls

  • 🕹️ WASD – Move
  • 🖱️ Mouse – Aim and shoot
  • 1–7 – Switch weapons (Flare Gun, Tommy Gun, Sawed-Off, etc.)
  • G – Throw dynamite or TNT
  • Space – Use/interact

🧩 How to Access & Play

  • 🗝️ Unlocking E4M9 – In Episode 4, Level 4 ("Crystal Lake"), find a hidden switch that opens a secret path to this level.
  • 🏬 Explore the Mall – Navigate shops, escalators, and ventilation shafts filled with cultists and undead.
  • 🧠 Find the Skull Key – Fight through K‑Marche to get the Skull Key behind a gargoyle.
  • 🔐 Unlock the Exit – Use vents and ramps to reach the security area and complete the level.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 💥 Use the Flare Gun and explosives to deal with zombies from a distance.
  • 🧃 Look for secrets—hidden areas contain Life Seed, Guns Akimbo, and more.
  • 🗺️ Don’t rush. Explore side rooms and vents to get better gear and survive ambushes.
  • 📸 This is a fan-favorite level—record your run and show off your score!
