Virtual Indie Expo 2025

Welcome to the most vibrant online celebration of indie games! The Virtual Indie Expo is a 4-day digital event that showcases innovation, art, and storytelling from independent developers across the globe.

From solo developers in their dorm rooms to indie studios pushing creative boundaries, this expo offers a front-row seat to the future of gaming. Participate from anywhere and enjoy a fully immersive digital experience!

  • 🗓️ Date: August 15–18, 2025
  • 🎮 Exhibits: Play 50+ curated indie games
  • 🎤 Live Panels: Talks by developers, artists, and streamers
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Meet the Makers: Live Q&A booths with dev teams
  • 🎁 Giveaways: Game keys, merchandise, and exclusive beta invites
  • 🌍 Languages: English, Spanish, Japanese, Hindi, and more

🌟 Special Feature: A “Rising Stars” section for debut games from student teams and first-time creators.

🎟️ Tickets: Free with registration. Optional donation tickets go toward indie dev grants!
