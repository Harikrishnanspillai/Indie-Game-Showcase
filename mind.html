About Mindustry

Mindustry is a hybrid tower-defense, real-time strategy, and factory-management game developed by Anuke. Build conveyor-based supply chains, craft ammo, defend your base from waves of enemies, and deploy combat units—all across grid-based maps and diverse planets. It’s open-source, moddable, and available on PC, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, and Steam :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

How to Play Mindustry

🕹️ Controls (PC)

  • W/S/A/D – Move and pan camera
  • Left Mouse – Place blocks / Shoot
  • Right Mouse – Remove blocks / Deselect
  • Scroll Wheel – Rotate block / Zoom in/out
  • Ctrl – Diagonal placement / zoom hold
  • Shift – Dash
  • Escape – Open menu
  • Space – Pause / unpause
  • M – Toggle minimap
  • P – Take map screenshot :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

🧩 Core Gameplay

  • ⛏️ Extract Resources – Place drill blocks on ore and route materials via conveyor belts to your Core.
  • 🏭 Build Factories & Turrets – Craft ammo and defenses to repel incoming enemy waves.
  • 🛡️ Survival Mode – Defend your Core from enemy waves on "Serpulo" or "Erekir" planets.
  • ⚔️ PvE Attacks & PvP – Attack enemy bases or engage in multiplayer PvP battles.
  • 📜 Campaign – Conquer sectors across planets, unlocking tech and bases.
  • 💡 Logic Scripting – Use logic blocks with MLog to automate production and defense :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}.
  • 🎮 Modding & Custom Maps – Use the map editor, mods, and schematics in sandbox and multiplayer :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⏸️ Pause before building to assess surroundings and plan defense :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}.
  • 🔁 Connect all production via conveyors—anything not linked to your Core or turrets won't work :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}.
  • 🤖 Start manual drilling with your ship to supplement automated drills.
  • 📘 Copy logic schematics from the community when you're learning scripting :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}:
    • “If you find logic confusing, i suggest copy or download logic schems… experiment on sandbox.” :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
  • 📱 On mobile, dragging conveyors takes some practice:
