About Pac-Man

Pac-Man is a timeless arcade game where you navigate a maze, eat pellets, and avoid ghosts. Your mission is to clear the maze while chasing high scores and power-ups!

How to Play Pac-Man

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️⬆️⬇️ Arrow Keys – Move Pac-Man in the maze
  • ⏸️ P – Pause the game (in some versions)

🧩 How to Play

  • 🍒 Eat Pellets – Clear the maze by eating all the small dots.
  • 👻 Avoid Ghosts – Don’t let the ghosts catch you!
  • 💪 Power Pellets – Eat large flashing dots to turn ghosts blue and eat them for bonus points.
  • 🎯 Chase High Scores – Collect fruits and eat ghosts for maximum points.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🗺️ Learn ghost patterns to survive longer.
  • 🚀 Use tunnels to escape when cornered.
  • 🔄 Save power pellets for emergencies or big combos.
  • 🏁 Finish faster for better time-based bonuses in some versions.
