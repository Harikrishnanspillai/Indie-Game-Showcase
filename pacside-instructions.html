About Pac-Side 👾🟡

Pac-Side is a creative twist on the classic Pac-Man game, blending side-scrolling mechanics with fast-paced maze action. Avoid enemies, collect pellets, and survive as long as you can!

How to Play 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • ⬅️➡️ Use Left and Right arrow keys to move.
    • ⬆️⬇️ Climb ladders or platforms if available.
    • 🍒 Collect pellets and bonuses to score points.

    🎯 Objective

    • ⚠️ Avoid Ghosts – Touching one ends your game.
    • 💡 Stay Alert – Enemies move fast and can trap you!
    • 🏆 High Score – Collect as many pellets as possible.

    🧠 Tips

    • 🚪 Look for escape routes in advance.
    • 👀 Watch enemy patterns and avoid dead ends.
    • 🔁 Practice to master the maze and improve timing.
