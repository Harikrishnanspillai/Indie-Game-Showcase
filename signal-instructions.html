About Signal Simulator

Signal Simulator is a unique simulation and horror experience where you play as a radio observatory technician hunting for signals from deep space. Maintain your equipment, decode transmissions, and uncover the mysteries lurking in the static.

How to Play Signal Simulator

🕹️ Controls

  • 🎮 WASD – Move around the station.
  • 🖱️ Mouse – Interact with terminals and tools.
  • 🔊 Keyboard – Type commands and calibrate signal towers.

📡 Gameplay Objectives

  • 📡 Scan the Skies – Control antennas to detect alien signals.
  • 💻 Manage Systems – Monitor power, repair modules, and decode data.
  • 🛸 Stay Safe – Strange things start happening... are you truly alone?

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🛠️ Don’t ignore errors — your systems can break down anytime.
  • 👽 Keep your eyes open for… unexpected visitors.
  • 💾 Save frequently — paranoia is part of the fun!
