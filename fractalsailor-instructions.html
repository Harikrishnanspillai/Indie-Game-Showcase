About Fractal Sailor 🌀⛵

Fractal Sailor is a mesmerizing indie game where you sail through beautiful, fractal-generated worlds. Navigate surreal patterns and unlock new regions while experiencing a peaceful, meditative journey.

How to Play Fractal Sailor 🌊

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use the mouse or arrow keys to steer your ship.
    • ⏩ Accelerate by holding down specific movement keys or mouse clicks.
    • 🌌 Zoom in and out to explore fractal environments.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🌍 Explore Fractals – Navigate through mesmerizing, infinite patterns.
    • 🚢 Control the Ship – Adjust speed and direction with precision.
    • 🔓 Unlock New Areas – Discover hidden realms within the fractals.

    💡 Tips for Players

    • 🎧 Use headphones for a calming audio experience.
    • 🧠 Don’t rush – the game rewards curiosity and patience.
    • 📷 Take screenshots of unique fractal patterns!
