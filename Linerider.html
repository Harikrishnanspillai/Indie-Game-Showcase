About Line Rider

Line Rider is a classic physics-based sandbox game where you draw lines to create a track for a sledder to ride. It’s all about creativity, timing, and making smooth or wild rides that your rider can survive!

How to Play Line Rider

🕹️ Controls

  • ✏️ Mouse – Draw tracks on the canvas
  • ▶️ Spacebar – Start and stop the sled
  • 🧽 Eraser Tool – Remove mistakes

🧩 How to Play

  • 🛷 Draw Your Track – Use the pencil tool to sketch a rideable line.
  • 🎢 Test the Ride – Press play and watch your sledder go!
  • 🧹 Fix and Improve – Use the eraser to clean up bumpy or broken tracks.
  • 🎨 Get Creative – Create jumps, loops, and epic rides.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 📐 Use different types of lines (normal, acceleration, scenery) to control motion and visuals.
  • 🛠️ Precision matters — smoother curves make better rides.
  • 📽️ Record and share your rides with others online!
  • 🌈 Add creativity — make scenes, stories, or music syncs.
