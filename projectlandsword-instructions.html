About Project Landsword ⚔️🌍

Project Landsword is a fast-paced action RPG that throws you into intense sword battles across mysterious lands. Dodge, slash, and explore in a pixel-art world full of challenges and surprises.

How to Play Project Landsword 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🎮 Use arrow keys or WASD to move.
    • 🖱️ Mouse click or key (e.g., space/enter) to attack.
    • ⇧ Shift or another key to dodge or block (if available).

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • ⚔️ Fight Enemies – Time your attacks and avoid damage.
    • 🗺️ Explore Levels – Discover new zones, enemies, and secrets.
    • 📦 Pick Up Items – Collect health, power-ups, and gear.
    • 💪 Get Stronger – Improve your skills and unlock new moves.

    💡 Tips

    • 👀 Watch enemy patterns carefully.
    • 🛡️ Use your dodge/block wisely in tough fights.
    • 🌟 Explore every corner for secrets and loot!
