About Shakedown Rally

Shakedown Rally is a fast-paced retro-style rally racing game with low-poly visuals and fun physics. Race across diverse terrains—muddy Kenya, snowy Sweden, the French Alps, and more—powered by a custom C++ engine. Easy to jump into, moddable, and optimized to run on any PC :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

How to Play Shakedown Rally

🕹️ Controls

  • W/S – Throttle and brake/reverse
  • A/D – Steer left and right
  • Enter – Toggle between automatic and manual gears
  • < / > – Shift gears manually
  • F – Flip back your car (+10 seconds penalty)
  • C – Switch camera view
