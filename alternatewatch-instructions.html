About Alternate Watch 👁️🕵️‍♂️

Alternate Watch is a spooky, observation-based horror game where you monitor a home for strange anomalies. Your job: spot the changes, report them, and survive the night!

How to Play 🔍

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to look around and interact.
    • 📸 Click "Report" to report anomalies when you spot them.

    🎯 Objective

    • 👀 Watch Carefully – Study every room and remember what belongs.
    • ⚠️ Spot Anomalies – Objects moving, changing, or disappearing? Report it!
    • 🕒 Survive the Night – Too many missed anomalies and it’s game over.

    🧠 Tips

    • 📝 Take mental notes or screenshots for reference.
    • 🧠 Trust your gut — if it feels off, report it.
    • 🔄 Recheck rooms often, anomalies can happen at any time.
