About Dungeons and Degenerate Gamblers 🎲🧙‍♂️

This is a roguelike dungeon crawler where luck is everything! Roll dice, bet, and battle your way through unpredictable rooms full of monsters, loot, and chaotic decisions!

How to Play 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Click to interact with cards, dice, and enemies.
    • 🎲 Drag and roll dice to attack or gamble.
    • 🧭 Use your mouse to explore the dungeon paths.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🎯 Roll Dice – Every action depends on the dice roll!
    • 🧃 Pick Up Items – Potions, buffs, and special dice help you survive longer.
    • ⚔️ Battle Enemies – Use lucky rolls to defeat foes in turn-based combat.
    • 🎰 Gamble Carefully – Some rooms give you a chance to bet and win powerful rewards.

    💡 Tips

    • 🧠 Plan your moves based on your dice outcomes.
    • 🕳️ Avoid traps and risky rolls if you’re low on health.
    • 💎 Use special dice strategically to gain the upper hand.
