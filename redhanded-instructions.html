About Red-Handed 🔍🕵️

Red-Handed is a short stealth-based puzzle game where you sneak through a crime scene, gather clues, and avoid being caught. Think fast and move carefully!

How to Play 🕹️

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to interact and move.
    • 🧭 Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate your character.

    🧠 Gameplay Guide

    • 🔎 Collect Clues – Find and gather important evidence.
    • 🕶️ Stay Hidden – Avoid security and cameras.
    • 🚪 Escape Safely – Once all clues are collected, reach the exit.

    💡 Tips

    • 🎯 Be precise with movements to avoid detection.
    • ⏱️ Time your actions around patrol patterns.
    • 🧩 Explore every corner — clues could be anywhere!
