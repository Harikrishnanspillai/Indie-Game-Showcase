About Future Grid ⚡🧠

Future Grid is a fast-paced, minimalist puzzle game where you connect circuits and solve logic challenges in a futuristic digital environment. Think ahead and light up the grid!

How to Play 🔧

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Click and drag to connect nodes and elements.
    • 🔄 Use your mouse to rotate or activate circuit parts (if applicable).

    🎯 Objective

    • 🔗 Connect Nodes – Link power sources to terminals through the grid.
    • 🧩 Plan Wisely – Some connections require specific paths or sequences.
    • 💡 Complete the Circuit – Solve the logic challenge to light up the grid and move on.

    🧠 Tips

    • 🔍 Look closely at each level’s layout before making moves.
    • ♻️ Try different paths if one doesn’t work — there’s always a solution.
    • 🧘 Stay calm and think logically — speed helps, but accuracy wins.
