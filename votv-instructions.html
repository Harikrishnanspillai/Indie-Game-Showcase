About Voice of the Void 🛰️

Voice of the Void is a first-person psychological horror game where you play as a scientist stationed in a remote research outpost. Strange cosmic signals are being received, and it's your job to decode them… or survive them.

How to Play Voice of the Void 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🎮 Move with WASD.
    • 🖱️ Use the mouse to look around.
    • 🔄 Interact with objects using E.
    • 📋 Check your tasks and logs often.

    🔍 Gameplay Guide

    • 📡 Monitor the signal equipment – Listen for anomalies.
    • 🔧 Maintain the base – Fix power, adjust antennas, and calibrate machines.
    • 🧠 Stay sane – The environment may affect your perception over time.
    • ❗ Follow protocols – Ignoring them may have consequences.

    🧠 Tips for Survival

    • 📻 Always monitor changes in the signal.
    • 🕯️ Stay in well-lit areas when possible.
    • 📘 Read logs and documents to piece together the truth.
