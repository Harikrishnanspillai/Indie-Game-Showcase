About Golf Peaks

Golf Peaks is a relaxing puzzle game where you use cards to move your golf ball across tricky, stylized terrain. It's less about golfing skill and more about logic and planning!

How to Play Golf Peaks

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Click on a card to use it.
  • 📍 Choose where to aim your shot based on the card’s action.

⛳ Gameplay Objectives

  • 🎯 Reach the Hole – Use a sequence of movement cards to guide your ball to the goal.
  • 🧩 Plan Moves – Cards have different types of movement: jumps, rolls, and more.
  • 📐 Use Logic – Think carefully about terrain and move limits.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 📋 Plan out your card usage before starting a level.
  • 🔁 Don’t be afraid to restart and rethink a puzzle.
  • 😌 Enjoy the calm music and relaxing pace — no timers, no pressure!
