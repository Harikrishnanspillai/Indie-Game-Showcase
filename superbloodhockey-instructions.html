About Super Blood Hockey

Super Blood Hockey is a brutal, violent, and darkly comedic arcade-style sports game. Take classic ice hockey, add buckets of pixelated gore, and challenge your skills in both local and tournament modes!

How to Play Super Blood Hockey

🕹️ Controls

  • 🎮 Arrow Keys / WASD – Move your player.
  • 🅱️ Z / Space – Pass or shoot the puck.
  • 🅰️ X / Ctrl – Body check your opponent.

🏒 Gameplay Objectives

  • 🥅 Score Goals – Use precise timing to outmaneuver and score against your opponents.
  • 💥 Fight to Win – Violence is part of the strategy. Injure rivals to gain advantage.
  • 🏆 Tournament Mode – Create your team and dominate the blood-soaked league.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⚠️ Injuries affect gameplay — protect your players.
  • 🧠 Use aggressive tactics to wear down teams over time.
  • 🎯 Aim for rebounds and open shots — accuracy matters!
