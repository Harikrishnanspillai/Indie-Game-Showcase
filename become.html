About We Become What We Behold

We Become What We Behold is a short, thought-provoking game that explores how media influences our perception of the world. As the player, you take on the role of a camera operator capturing moments that shape society — for better or worse.

How to Play We Become What We Behold

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Click to capture moments on camera
  • 🎯 Aim – Move your cursor over scenes to focus the camera

🧩 How to Play

  • 📸 Take Pictures – Capture key events by clicking on people or interactions.
  • 📰 Influence the Crowd – Your photos are broadcasted and affect how people behave.
  • 🔁 Observe Reactions – Watch how the public slowly changes based on the media you present.
  • 🎭 Shape the Story – Your choices lead to an impactful ending. What story will you tell?

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🕵️ Pay close attention to subtle changes in character behavior.
  • 📷 Don’t just snap everything — timing and subject matter matter!
  • 💥 Small events can spiral into big changes.
  • 🧠 The game is short but powerful — play it slowly and reflect.
