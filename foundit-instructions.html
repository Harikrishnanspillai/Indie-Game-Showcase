About Found It 🔍🧠

"Found It" is a relaxing and casual hidden object game where your goal is to search for specific items in a colorful and lively scene. Perfect for players who love puzzles and attention-to-detail games!

How to Play Found It 🕹️

    🖱️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to move around the scene.
    • 👆 Click on hidden objects to collect them.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 📜 Check the Object List – Find the items shown on the list.
    • 👀 Look Carefully – Some objects are cleverly hidden!
    • 🎯 Click to Collect – Just click the item once you find it.

    💡 Tips

    • 🧘 Play calmly – there's no time limit!
    • 🔍 Zoom in (if supported) to spot tiny objects.
    • 📦 Some items may be partially hidden behind others.
