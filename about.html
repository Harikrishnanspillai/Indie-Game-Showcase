About Indie Game Showcase

🌟 What is This Website?

This is a platform built to spotlight and support independent (indie) game developers. It showcases creative and unique games that often get overshadowed by big studios. Users can explore games, view trailers, play demos, and even rate them!

🎯 Why We Made This Website?

Indie games are incredibly creative but rarely receive attention. We created this website to help them reach the spotlight they deserve — a space by gamers, for gamers.

🏆 Purpose & Goals

  • To promote amazing indie developers and their games.
  • To give users a fun, interactive space to discover unique games.
  • To build a modern, responsive, and creative web project.
  • To gain hands-on experience with teamwork and web development tools.
