About Xenospore 🧬

Xenospore is a sci-fi survival strategy game where you play as a researcher on a distant planet, studying and containing an alien spore outbreak. Expand your base, study the spores, and make decisions that affect the outcome of your mission.

How to Play Xenospore 🚀

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to interact with the environment.
    • 📦 Click on buildings, units, or menus to manage resources and actions.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🔬 Research the Spores – Study alien biology to unlock tools.
    • 🏗️ Build and Expand – Create labs, living quarters, and defenses.
    • 💡 Manage Resources – Balance power, supplies, and manpower.
    • 🧠 Make Tough Choices – Your decisions impact the story and survival.

    📌 Tips for Success

    • ⚠️ Monitor spore spread frequently.
    • 🔋 Don't run out of power — prioritize energy systems.
    • 🧪 Research early and often to gain new advantages.
