About Ludo King

Ludo King is a digital adaptation of the classic board game Ludo. Designed for multiplayer fun, it combines strategy and luck. Play with friends, family, or online players and race your tokens to the finish before others do!

How to Play Ludo King

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Click to roll the dice and select tokens
  • 📲 Touch – On mobile, tap to play

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎲 Roll the Dice – Each turn starts with a dice roll.
  • 🚀 Start with a Six – Roll a six to bring your tokens into play.
  • 🏃 Move Tokens – Move your tokens strategically around the board.
  • 🎯 Capture Opponents – Land on an opponent’s token to send them back to start.
  • 🏁 Reach Home – Get all your tokens to the home triangle first to win!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🧮 Plan ahead to avoid getting captured.
  • 🔄 Use your sixes wisely — you get another turn!
  • 🛡️ Stay safe in colored zones where you can’t be attacked.
  • 💡 Keep your tokens spaced to reduce risk.
