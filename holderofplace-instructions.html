About Holder of Place 🪑

Holder of Place is a short narrative-driven game where you play as someone hired to "hold a place" in a long queue. What seems like a simple task becomes a quiet reflection on time, space, and purpose.

How to Play Holder of Place 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to interact with objects and people.
    • ⌨️ Use keyboard inputs as prompted during dialogues.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🗣️ Engage with characters – Talk to others in the queue to explore different perspectives.
    • ⏳ Be patient – The story unfolds slowly, emphasizing stillness and thought.
    • 🧘 Reflect – The game is about mood and meaning, not action or puzzles.

    💡 Tips for Players

    • 🎧 Play with sound for a full immersive experience.
    • 🕰️ Take your time and don’t rush – this game is meditative.
    • 🖼️ Pay attention to visuals and text for hidden meanings.
