About Temple Run 2

Temple Run 2 is an endless running game that tests your reflexes and agility. Escape from the cursed temple while dodging obstacles, jumping over gaps, and collecting coins. It's a fast-paced adventure that gets more thrilling the longer you run!

How to Play Temple Run 2

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Swipe with mouse or keyboard arrows to control the runner
  • 📲 Touch – Swipe up/down/left/right on mobile to jump, slide, and turn

🧩 How to Play

  • 🏃 Run Automatically – Your character runs on their own.
  • ↩️ Swipe to Turn – Swipe left or right to make turns at intersections.
  • ⬆️ Jump – Swipe up to leap over obstacles.
  • ⬇️ Slide – Swipe down to slide under barriers.
  • 💰 Collect Coins – Gather coins to unlock characters and upgrades.
  • 🔥 Avoid Hazards – Dodge fire, gaps, spinning blades, and cliffs!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⚡ React quickly to sudden turns and obstacles.
  • 🧲 Use power-ups to boost your run and collect more coins.
  • 💎 Collect gems to revive after a fall or crash.
  • 🎯 Stay focused — the longer you survive, the faster it gets!
