About Slope

Slope is a thrilling 3D endless running game where you control a ball rolling down a steep, neon-lit slope. The game gets faster and harder the longer you survive — dodge obstacles, stay on track, and aim for the high score!

How to Play Slope

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️ Left Arrow – Move Left
  • ➡️ Right Arrow – Move Right

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎮 Start Rolling – The ball rolls automatically down the slope.
  • ⚡ Dodge Obstacles – Avoid red blocks and gaps in the track.
  • 🏃 React Fast – The speed increases the longer you survive.
  • 📈 Score High – Survive as long as possible to beat your record!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🌀 Don’t overcorrect — gentle movements are more effective.
  • 🧱 Stay away from the edges and red blocks.
  • 🕹️ The game’s physics are fast — anticipate turns early!
  • 🎯 Focus on the center path for easier control.
