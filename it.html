About Lancer Tactics

Lancer Tactics is a tactical RPG where you command customizable mechs in grid-based combat. Deploy units, exploit terrain, and draw on advanced weaponry to outsmart your foes. It's a challenging strategy experience that rewards planning, skill, and stylish maneuvers!
