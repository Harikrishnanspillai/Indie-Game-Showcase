About Lancer Tactics

Lancer Tactics is a tactical RPG where you command customizable mechs in grid-based combat. Deploy units, exploit terrain, and draw on advanced weaponry to outsmart your foes. It's a challenging strategy experience that rewards planning, skill, and stylish maneuvers!

How to Play Lancer Tactics

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Click to select units, move, and attack
  • ⌨️ Keyboard – Use Q/E to prioritize abilities, Z/X to turn camera

🧩 How to Play

  • 🔍 Select a Unit – Click on a mech to view its stats and options.
  • 🎯 Move – Click on a highlighted tile to reposition your mech.
  • 🔫 Attack or Use Ability – Choose a target within range.
  • 🛡️ Defend or Overwatch – Use cover or guard against enemy moves.
  • ⏭️ End Turn – All your units act before passing to the enemy.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🏔️ Position units on high ground for accuracy bonuses.
  • 🧩 Combine unit abilities—support, tanks, and snipers work best together.
  • ⚙️ Customize load-outs between missions; adapt weapons and mods to the enemy.
  • 📊 Monitor heat levels to avoid overheating during critical moments.
