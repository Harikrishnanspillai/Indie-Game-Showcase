About SkateBIRD 🛹🐦

SkateBIRD is a charming skateboarding game where you play as a tiny bird shredding around desk parks and office ramps. Do tricks, flap around, and have fun on your feathered four-wheeler!

How to Play SkateBIRD 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • ⬅️➡️ Use arrow keys or WASD to move and steer.
    • 🅱️ Press spacebar to ollie (jump).
    • 🎯 Use trick buttons (check controls guide in-game) to perform flips and spins.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 📋 Complete Objectives – Follow mini missions and goals in each level.
    • 🎮 Do Tricks – Grind rails, do kickflips, and earn high scores.
    • 📦 Explore Levels – Unlock cool areas by progressing through challenges.

    💡 Tips

    • 🐣 Don’t worry about falling — birds always bounce back!
    • 🎧 Customize your bird with accessories for style points.
    • ⏱️ Timing your ollies helps you chain tricks better.
