About Trigaea 🌌

Trigaea is a sci-fi interactive story game where your decisions shape the outcome. You explore alien worlds, piece together your past, and interact with a mysterious AI companion through a branching narrative.

How to Play Trigaea 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Click to select dialogue options or actions.
    • ⌨️ Use arrow keys or spacebar for choices, if available.

    📜 Game Flow

    • 📖 Read the Story – Follow the text closely to understand the world and your role.
    • 🧠 Make Choices – Your responses and decisions affect the story's path.
    • 💾 Save Progress – Save your journey to explore different endings later.

    🧠 Pro Tips

    • 🔍 Pay attention to memory fragments.
    • 🤖 Listen to your AI – it helps uncover the truth.
    • 🔄 Try replaying for alternate endings and secrets.
