Indie Game Dev Bootcamp

Dreaming of making your own game but don’t know where to start? The Game Dev Bootcamp is a one-week beginner-friendly intensive that takes you from zero to prototype using industry tools and techniques.

Whether you're a student, hobbyist, or aspiring professional, this bootcamp gives you hands-on experience with real-world workflows. By the end, you'll have your own playable game and a solid foundation to grow as a developer.

  • 🗓️ Date: September 2–9, 2025
  • ⏰ Daily Sessions: 3 hours of guided learning + 2 hours of project work
  • 🧠 Topics Covered:
    • Game Design Fundamentals
    • Intro to Unity & Godot
    • Pixel Art and Animation Basics
    • Scripting in C#
    • Publishing to Itch.io
  • 👨‍🏫 Instructors: Veteran indie developers and educators
  • 📜 Certificate: Completion certificate + project showcase
  • 🎓 Bonus: Portfolio review and mentorship hours included

💬 Community access: You’ll also join a dedicated Discord channel with fellow bootcampers and mentors for support, feedback, and motivation.

🎟️ Seats are limited – Register by August 25 to secure your spot!
