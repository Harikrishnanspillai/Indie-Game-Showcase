About Poly Track

Poly Track is a fast-paced 3D racing game set in a low-poly world. Race through dynamic tracks, avoid obstacles, and try to beat the clock. It’s a fun blend of speed, reaction, and colorful graphics — perfect for casual gamers and adrenaline junkies alike!

How to Play Poly Track

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️ Arrow Keys – Steer your car left and right
  • ⏩ Up Arrow – Accelerate
  • ⏬ Down Arrow – Brake
  • ⎋ Esc – Pause/Exit menu

🧩 How to Play

  • 🏁 Start the Race – Choose a track and get ready to go!
  • 🚗 Control Your Vehicle – Navigate twists and turns at high speeds.
  • ⚠️ Avoid Obstacles – Steer clear of barriers and sharp curves.
  • ⏱️ Beat the Timer – Complete the track before time runs out.
  • 🏆 Set High Scores – Try to beat your own records!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🎯 Stay centered on narrow paths to avoid falling.
  • 💨 Use speed boosts wisely — control is key!
  • 🧠 Memorize tricky turns to improve lap times.
  • 🔄 Play again to perfect your timing and pathing.
