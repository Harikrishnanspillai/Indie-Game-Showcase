About Krunker.io

Krunker.io is a fast-paced, browser-based first-person shooter where players compete in quick matches across various maps and game modes. With pixel-style graphics and smooth controls, Krunker offers competitive gameplay with dozens of weapons and classes.

How to Play Krunker.io

🕹️ Controls

  • 🔫 Left Click – Shoot
  • 🎯 Right Click – Aim (if using scope weapons)
  • 🕹️ W/A/S/D – Move
  • ⏎ Space – Jump
  • 🎮 Shift – Crouch/slide
  • 🔄 R – Reload
  • 🎒 1/2 – Switch weapons

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎮 Join a Match – Start in public lobbies or custom games with other players.
  • 🧍 Choose a Class – From Triggerman to Sniper, each has unique stats and weapons.
  • 🔫 Eliminate Opponents – Earn points by defeating other players and surviving.
  • 🏆 Climb the Leaderboard – Get the most kills to rank #1 before the timer runs out!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🔥 Practice flick shots and movement for an edge.
  • 🎯 Headshots deal more damage – aim high.
  • 🗺️ Learn maps to control chokepoints and ambush spots.
  • ⚙️ Customize controls and sensitivity in settings.
