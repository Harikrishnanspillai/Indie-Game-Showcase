About High Stakes 🧛💰

High Stakes is a clever, fast-paced card-based strategy game where you play as a vampire trying to escape a mansion while avoiding sunlight and collecting loot. It's all about risk, timing, and survival!

How to Play High Stakes 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Use your mouse to select cards and make moves.
    • 🧭 Navigate the mansion by choosing your path from available card options.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🌞 Avoid Sunlight – Don’t step into sunlit areas or you'll be burned!
    • 💵 Collect Gold – Grab as much treasure as you can while staying alive.
    • 🗝️ Use Items Wisely – Some cards give you abilities or escape options.
    • ⚠️ Think Ahead – Every card you play affects your route and fate.

    💡 Tips

    • 🧠 Plan moves carefully — one wrong step can be fatal.
    • 💼 Risk vs Reward — sometimes it’s better to escape with less loot!
    • 🔄 Replay often to improve and try new strategies.
