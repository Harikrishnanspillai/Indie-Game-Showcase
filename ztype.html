About ZType

ZType is an action-packed typing game where each keystroke launches a missile! Type the words on incoming enemy ships to destroy them before they reach your base. It’s a fun way to boost typing speed and reflexes.

How to Play ZType

🕹️ Controls

  • ⌨️ Keyboard – Type letters to fire at enemy words

🧩 How to Play

  • 🚀 Start the Game – Enemies appear from the top with words attached.
  • 🔤 Target Enemies – Begin typing a word to lock onto a specific ship.
  • 💥 Fire Automatically – Each correct keystroke launches a missile. Finish the word to destroy the enemy.
  • 🎮 Advance Levels – Survive waves of ships and progress through more challenging levels.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🎯 Focus on one enemy at a time to avoid distractions.
  • ⏱️ Type smoothly and accurately — speed will follow.
  • 🧘 Stay calm under pressure to prevent typos.
  • 🧠 Practicing daily helps improve typing and reaction time.
