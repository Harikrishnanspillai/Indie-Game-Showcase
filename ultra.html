About Ultrakill Prelude

Ultrakill Prelude is a retro-style, ultra-fast-paced FPS inspired by classic shooters like Doom and Quake. Blast your way through hordes of enemies using a variety of weapons, stylish movement mechanics, and brutal combos. Speed, precision, and aggression are your tools for survival!

How to Play Ultrakill Prelude

🕹️ Controls

  • 🕹️ WASD – Move
  • 🖱️ Mouse – Aim and shoot
  • 🔄 Scroll Wheel / Number Keys – Switch weapons
  • 🔫 Right Click – Alternate fire (varies by weapon)
  • ␣ Space – Jump
  • ⇧ Shift – Dash
  • Ctrl – Slide

🧩 How to Play

  • 🔥 Kill Fast, Stay Alive – Destroy enemies with speed and style to stay alive.
  • 💉 Heal Through Damage – Absorb blood from enemies to restore health.
  • 🔁 Switch Weapons – Use the best weapon for the situation.
  • 🏃 Master Movement – Dash, slide, and wall-jump to avoid damage and stay mobile.
  • 🧱 Explore – Find hidden rooms and secrets for extra rewards.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🔄 Chain combos and keep moving — standing still is a death sentence.
  • 🧛 Heal by getting close and dealing damage — don't run away!
  • 🧠 Each enemy type has a weakness — learn and exploit them.
  • 💥 Use style points to track your performance — aim for that S rank!
