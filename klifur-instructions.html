About Klifur

Klifur is a fast-paced arcade climbing game where you control a nimble adventurer scaling treacherous cliffs! Your goal? Reach as high as you can without falling. Simple to learn, tough to master — perfect for speedrunners and thrill-seekers.

How to Play Klifur

🕹️ Controls

  • ⬅️➡️ Use arrow keys to move left and right.
  • 🧗 Press spacebar to jump between handholds.
  • ⏫ Hold spacebar for longer jumps — but time it right!

🧗 Game Objective

  • 🏔️ Climb as high as you can without falling!
  • ⏱️ Beat your personal high score or race against friends.
  • ⚠️ Watch out for falling rocks, slippery holds, and collapsing ledges.

🎯 Pro Tips

  • 🧠 Precision > Speed: Rushing leads to falls!
  • 🎮 Try using a controller for smoother control.
  • 🏆 Experiment with different routes — some are faster, others safer.
