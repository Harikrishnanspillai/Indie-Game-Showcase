About Kurukshetra: Ascension

Kurukshetra: Ascension is a futuristic turn-based strategy game inspired by Indian mythology. Play as legendary warriors and tacticians, and engage in intense tactical battles where your skills and planning determine the outcome.

How to Play Kurukshetra: Ascension

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Use mouse to select units and move on the battlefield.
  • ⚔️ Click on enemies to attack or use abilities.
  • 🔄 End your turn when all actions are complete.

🎮 Gameplay Overview

  • 🧙‍♂️ Select Your Heroes – Choose from mythological characters with unique powers.
  • 🌌 Plan Strategically – Use terrain and timing to outsmart your foes.
  • 🏆 Ascend – Defeat waves of enemies and progress through divine realms.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⚡ Use abilities wisely — they have cooldowns!
  • 💠 Positioning is key — flank enemies for extra damage.
  • 🌟 Upgrade wisely — each level-up choice affects your strategy.
