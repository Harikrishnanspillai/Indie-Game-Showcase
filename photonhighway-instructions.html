About Photon Highway 🚀🌌

Photon Highway is a fast-paced, neon-lit racing game where you speed through an endless cosmic highway, dodging obstacles and collecting energy. It's all about reflexes and rhythm!

How to Play 🕹️

    🎮 Controls

    • ⬅️➡️ Use Left/Right Arrow Keys to steer your photon ship.
    • ⏫ Avoid obstacles and stay on the glowing track.

    🌟 Gameplay Guide

    • ⚡ Collect Energy Orbs – Boost your score by grabbing orbs!
    • 🚧 Dodge Obstacles – Hitting barriers ends your run.
    • 🎶 Ride the Beat – The game may sync with music rhythm!

    💡 Tips

    • 👀 Focus ahead – Obstacles appear quickly!
    • 🎯 Stay centered when possible for faster reaction.
    • 🔥 The longer you survive, the faster it gets!
