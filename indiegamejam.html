Indie Game Jam 2025

Get ready for a thrilling 48-hour global showdown where indie developers, artists, and storytellers unite to create incredible games from scratch!

This year’s Indie Game Jam 2025 a weekend packed with creativity, caffeine, and community. Whether you're a veteran developer or a first-time jammer, this event offers a welcoming space to experiment and express your wildest game ideas.

  • 🗓️ Date: July 20–22, 2025
  • 🕒 Duration: 48 hours non-stop
  • 🌐 Format: Online (Discord + Itch.io)
  • 🎨 Theme: Revealed at start of jam
  • 🏆 Judging Criteria: Creativity, Fun, Polish, Use of Theme
  • 💬 Mentor Sessions: Game design, Unity tips, pixel art, and more
  • 🎁 Prizes: Feature on our homepage, sponsor swag, and more!

💡Pro Tip: You can jam solo or form a team. Networking events before the jam help you find teammates with matching skills!

📢 Registration closes on July 15th. Don’t miss your chance to join the jam!
