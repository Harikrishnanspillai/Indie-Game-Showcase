About Skribbl.io

Skribbl.io is a free online multiplayer drawing and guessing game. One player draws a word, and the others try to guess it in real-time. It’s a fun way to test your drawing skills and word-guessing speed with friends or players around the world!

How to Play Skribbl.io

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Click and drag to draw, select colors and tools
  • ⌨️ Keyboard – Type your guesses into the chat box

🧩 How to Play

  • 🎨 Take Turns Drawing – One player draws while others guess.
  • 🔤 Guess the Word – Type in your guess before the time runs out.
  • ⏱️ Time Limit – Each round has a countdown timer.
  • 📝 Word Choices – The drawer chooses one of three random words.
  • 🏆 Score Points – The faster you guess, the more points you earn!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 👁️ Pay attention to the number of letters and correct letters shown.
  • 🎯 Be fast — early guesses earn more points.
  • 🧑‍🎨 Don’t overcomplicate your drawing. Keep it simple and clear!
  • 💬 Keep an eye on the chat — you might spot useful clues from others' guesses.
