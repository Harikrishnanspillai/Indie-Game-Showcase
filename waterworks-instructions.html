About Waterworks

Waterworks is a fun stickman strategy game where you control an army and solve challenges to protect your water supply. Use smart moves to defend, attack, and survive in a creative world full of obstacles. Perfect for players who love action and planning!

How to Play Waterworks!

  • 🎯 Your goal is to bring water to all the buildings.
  • 🃏 Each turn, you draw cards like pumps, pipes, and workers.
  • 🛠️ Use cards to build water systems across the town.
  • 💧 Make sure each house turns blue (has water).
  • 🔥 Put out fires quickly using fire-fighting cards.
  • 📉 Keep your Influence high to avoid losing.
  • 🏆 You win when all buildings have water!
