🎯 Indie Game Trivia

🎮

Celeste was made in just 4 days during a game jam!

🎵

Undertale's soundtrack was composed by Toby Fox in FL Studio.

👾

Hollow Knight was mostly developed by just two people.

🌱

Stardew Valley was created by one developer — ConcernedApe — over four years.

🧠

Baba Is You lets you rewrite the game rules while playing.

🌌

Hyper Light Drifter's story is based on the developer’s personal health issues.

⚙️

Papers, Please was made by one person, Lucas Pope, in under a year.

🔥

Limbo's art was made entirely in black and white to create mood.

🔍

Return of the Obra Dinn uses a unique 1-bit visual style.

🏗️

Fez took over five years to complete due to design complexity.

👁️

INSIDE has no dialogue but tells a powerful story through visuals alone.

🕹️

Katana ZERO features a built-in level editor used by the devs during testing.

💡

Oxenfree's dialogue system was praised for its natural flow and branching.

⏳

TimeLoop mechanics have become a popular design trend in indies.

🎤

OneShot is known for breaking the fourth wall during gameplay.

🧊

A Short Hike was made in just 3 months and became a cozy hit.

🔮

Dicey Dungeons combines dice mechanics with rogue-like strategy.

🎼

Thomas Was Alone uses narration and music to give emotion to simple blocks.

🌍

Don't Starve blends survival with Tim Burton–style visuals.

🚀

FTL: Faster Than Light helped launch the roguelike boom in indies.

🪙

Terraria has over 5,000 unique items — all crafted by a small indie team.

💀

Dead Cells was created by just a handful of devs — and has no jump button cooldown!

💣

Super Meat Boy was developed by just two people and inspired a documentary.

🧵

Little Misfortune is voiced by the actual game developer!

📼

Five Nights at Freddy’s was created as a comeback after negative reviews on a previous game.

💬

Kind Words is an indie game where all you do is send comforting messages to real people.

⚔️

Gris won awards for both its soundtrack and hand-drawn art style.

🎯

Geometry Dash was made by just one developer and features music-synced gameplay.

🌠

Journey, though widely popular, started as a small independent concept.

📖

To The Moon was created entirely in RPG Maker but tells an emotional story stronger than many AAA games.
