About Exo-Observation

Exo-Observation is a narrative exploration game where you monitor a mysterious planet from afar. Using observation tools, decode the alien world's story through subtle details, patterns, and transmissions.

How to Play Exo-Observation

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Mouse – Interact with instruments and monitor panels.
  • 🔍 Click to zoom or focus on signals and visual anomalies.

📡 Gameplay Objectives

  • 👁️ Observe Carefully – Watch and listen closely to incoming data from the alien world.
  • 📖 Uncover the Story – Interpret signals, visuals, and environmental clues to piece together the planet’s mysteries.
  • 🧠 Think Critically – Pay attention to timelines, anomalies, and subtle changes.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 👂 Use headphones for an immersive signal decoding experience.
  • 📝 Jot down clues — the game rewards careful observation.
  • 🛰️ Some events only happen once. Be attentive!
