Developer Q&A Sessions

Ever wanted to ask your favorite indie game creators about their journey, struggles, or secret sauce behind their game design? Here's your chance!

The Developer Q&A Sessions is a monthly live stream series that gives fans and fellow devs a peek into the minds behind some of the most creative indie games. It’s casual, unscripted, and full of insights.

  • 🗓️ Schedule: Last Saturday of every month
  • 🎙️ Format: 1-hour live stream with audience questions
  • 🎤 Guests: New dev team every month – from pixel platformers to VR visionaries
  • 💬 Live Chat: Ask questions in real-time
  • 📹 Watch Later: All sessions archived on our YouTube channel

🤝 Bonus: Join the pre-stream networking circle to meet fellow developers and fans!

📝 Want to be featured? Submit your dev team for a spotlight session by filling out our interest form.
