About Celeste Classic

Celeste Classic is a fast-paced platformer where you help Madeline climb a dangerous mountain. Originally made in just 4 days for a game jam, it became the foundation for the full Celeste game. Precision, timing, and quick reflexes are key to mastering it!

How to Play Celeste Classic

🕹️ Controls

  • ◀️▶️ Arrow Keys – Move left and right
  • ⬆️ or Z – Jump
  • X – Dash in 8 directions
  • R – Restart level

🧩 How to Play

  • ⛰️ Climb the Mountain – Navigate through challenging levels filled with spikes, gaps, and traps.
  • ⚡ Use Dash Wisely – Dash can be used once in mid-air before landing or grabbing a wall.
  • 🧱 Wall Grab – Stick to walls and climb up or slide down to plan your next move.
  • 💎 Collect Strawberries – Optional challenge for skilled players, showing your mastery.

🧠 Pro Tips

  • 🎯 Precision is everything – time your jumps and dashes carefully.
  • 🧗 Practice wall jumping and air dashing to chain moves together.
  • 💡 Each screen is a puzzle — don’t rush blindly.
  • 📈 It’s okay to fail — you restart instantly, so keep trying!
