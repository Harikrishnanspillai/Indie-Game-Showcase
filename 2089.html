About Vecktor 2089

Vecktor 2089 is a futuristic anti-gravity racing game featuring high-speed loops, adaptive AI, and customizable ships. Race solo or with friends on impossible tracks set to an awesome soundtrack by Dubmood!

How to Play Vecktor 2089

🕹️ Controls

  • 🖱️ Keyboard/Controller – Steer your ship
  • 🎮 Gamepad support – Fully compatible

🧩 How to Play

  • 🏎️ Select Ship & Class – Choose from 5 ships and 4 speed classes.
  • 📈 Start the Race – Compete against AI on looped tracks.
  • 🧠 Adaptive AI – Opponents adjust to your skill level.
  • 🕰️ Time Trials & Replays – Race solo, watch replays, and track your speed graph.
  • 👥 Local Multiplayer – Play split-screen with up to 4 players!

🧠 Pro Tips

  • ⚙️ Tweak AI difficulty to match your skill, especially in local multiplayer.
  • 🎧 Enjoy the fast-paced soundtrack—it helps set the pace!
  • 🧩 Use the built‑in track and ship editors to personalize your experience.
  • 🎯 Save a GIF (F9) of your best runs and share them with #vektor2089.
