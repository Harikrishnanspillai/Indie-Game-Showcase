About Good Job 👔🏢

Good Job is a light-hearted puzzle game where you play as the boss’s child trying to complete office tasks in the most chaotic or clever way possible. Crash, solve, and laugh your way up the corporate ladder!

How to Play 🕹️

    🎮 Controls

    • 🎯 Use arrow keys or WASD to move.
    • 🖱️ Click or space to interact with objects.
    • 🚪 Push, pull, or launch items to solve puzzles.

    🧩 Gameplay Guide

    • 🧠 Think Creatively – There’s no single solution to tasks.
    • 💥 Break Things – Sometimes chaos is the fastest way.
    • 📦 Use Office Supplies – Everything can be useful!

    💡 Tips

    • 🏃 Move fast, but don’t forget to experiment.
    • 🧹 Clean up for bonus points — or don’t 😄
    • 🏆 Each level has multiple ways to complete it — replay and discover!
