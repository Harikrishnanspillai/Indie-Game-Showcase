About The Hive 🐝🌌

The Hive is a strategic, atmospheric game where you control a mysterious hive mind, expand your influence, and uncover secrets in a surreal world. Guide your swarm with precision and intuition.

How to Play 🕹️

    🎮 Controls

    • 🖱️ Click to move your swarm or interact with objects.
    • 🧠 Guide hivelings toward objectives using point-and-click mechanics.

    🌐 Gameplay Guide

    • 🔍 Explore Environments – Discover new areas and evolve.
    • 💥 Avoid Hazards – Don’t let your swarm get destroyed.
    • 📖 Uncover Lore – Find fragments of the world’s story.

    💡 Tips

    • 👁️ Patience is key — observe before acting.
    • 🌟 Use terrain wisely to shield your swarm.
    • 🧬 Think like a hive — plan collectively.
