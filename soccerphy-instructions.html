About Soccer Physics ⚽🕹️

Soccer Physics is a hilarious and chaotic one-button sports game where the players flop, flip, and fly! Score goals using simple controls and physics-based movement—perfect for quick fun matches with friends.

How to Play 🎮

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ / 🔘 Press one button to make both your players jump forward.
    • 🧍‍♂️ Each press makes them hop or kick randomly based on physics!
    • 👥 Play solo or with a friend (local multiplayer).

    ⚽ Gameplay Guide

    • 🎯 Score Goals – Get the ball into the opponent’s net.
    • 💥 Use Timing – Time your jumps for powerful hits or tricky moves.
    • 🌀 Watch the Chaos – Each round has different physics (slippery floor, bouncy ball, etc.).

    💡 Tips

    • 😂 Embrace the madness – things will get weird!
    • 🧠 Try to predict the bounce of the ball.
    • 👊 Use body slams to knock the ball hard!
