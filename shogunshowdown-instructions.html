About Shogun Showdown

Shogun Showdown is a tactical turn-based roguelike where you fight using cards and smart positioning on a tight battlefield. Time your attacks, upgrade your deck, and outmaneuver enemies in a stylish pixel-art samurai world.

How to Play Shogun Showdown!

    🕹️ Controls

    • 🖱️ Click on cards to use them.
    • ⬅️➡️ Move left or right using your movement cards.
    • ⚔️ Use attack cards to strike enemies in front of you.

    🧩 How to Play

    • 🔢 Take Turns – You go first, then the enemies move.
    • 🗺️ Move Strategically – Use movement cards to dodge and line up attacks.
    • ⚔️ Attack Smartly – Different cards have different patterns.
    • 💡 Think Ahead – Enemies show their next move, so plan carefully.
    • ⏳ Charge Your Cards – Some need time before they’re ready.
    • 📦 Collect Upgrades – Strengthen your deck after each battle.
    • ❤️ Don’t Lose All Health – Keep your hearts safe!

    🧠 Pro Tips

    • ♻️ Combine cards for combos.
    • 📈 Always check enemy plans before acting.
    • 🧙‍♂️ Upgrade wisely based on your play style.
